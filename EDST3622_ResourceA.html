Welcome to your future career!

Prepare for the world of work by mastering essential vocabulary and learning how to express your intentions (つもりです), purposes (ために), and desires (たいです).

How to play the 6 Stages:

  • Stage 1 (Vocabulary): 3 rounds of matching (Hiragana to English, 3-column Kanji matching, and Emoji contexts).
  • Stage 2 (Guided Chunking): Translate English to Japanese by assembling sentence chunks.
  • Stage 3 (Reverse Translation): Translate Japanese sentences back into English.
  • Stage 4 (Independent Application): Build Japanese sentences word-by-word (watch out for distractors!).
  • Stage 5 (Advanced Reverse): Translate complex Japanese sentences containing intentions back into English.
  • Stage 6 (Reading Comprehension): Read an interview transcript and answer multiple-choice questions.
